Chicken Alfredo

This delicious Chicken Alfredo comes from the greatest of minds out there ChatGPT so you know that this recipe will be out of this world!

Ingredients

  • 8 Ounces of Fettuccine Pasta
  • 2 Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breasts
  • 2 Tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 4 Garlic Cloves, Minced
  • 1 Cup of Heavy Cream
  • 1 Cup of Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup of Unsalted Butter
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste
  • Chopped Fresh Parsley, for Garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the fettucine pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil oover medium heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook for about 6-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through and no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the skillet and let it rest for a few minutes. Slice the cchicken into thin strips.
  3. In the same skillet add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute, until fragrant. Be careful not to burn it.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and add the heavy cream to the skillet. Stir well to combine with the garlic.
  5. Add the butter to the skillet and still until melted and fully incorporated into the cream. Gradually whisk in the grated parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  6. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste. If the sauce becomes too thick, you can add a little more cream or pasta cooking water to thin it out.
  7. Add the cooked fettuccine pasta to the skillet and toss until it is fully coated with the Alfredo sauce.
  8. Add the sliced chicken to the skillet and gently toss to combine, ensuring the chicken is evenly distribute throughout the pasta.
  9. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley and additional grated parmesan cheese, if desired
  10. Serve the Chicken Alfredo hot and enjoy!

Note: You can also add vegetables like steamed broccoli or sauteed mushrooms to the pasta for extra flavor and texture.

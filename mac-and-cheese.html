Baked Mac and Cheese

I bet you have not tried any baked mac and cheese recipes as delicious and cheesiest as this one. This mac and cheese is not for the lactose intollerant as it will contain lots of cheese, perfect for those instagram pictures!

Ingredients

  • 8 Ounces of Elbow Macaroni
  • 4 Tablespoons of Unsalted Butter
  • 1/4 Cups of All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 Cups of Milk
  • 1 Cup of Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Cup of Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1/2 Teaspoons of Salt
  • 1/4 Teaspoons of Black Pepper
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Paprika(optional)
  • 1/4 Cup of Grated Parmesan Cheese(optional, for topping)
  • Chopped Fresh Parsley, for Garnishing(optional)
